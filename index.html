Understanding Techno Rhythms

Four-on-the-Floor

The foundation of techno: a kick drum on every quarter note. This driving rhythm creates forward momentum.

Off-Beat Hi-Hats

Closed hi-hats on off-beats create energy, while open hi-hats add release and tension.

Accent & Velocity

Highlighted beats are accented - played louder. This creates groove and dynamics.

Accent Techniques

Higher velocity, layered highpass hits, or subtle pitch shifts. Each creates different textures.

MIDI Note Mapping

Each drum has unique accent notes for DAW customization. Map to custom samples for creative results.

TR-909 Legacy

These patterns are inspired by the legendary Roland TR-909, the drum machine that defined electronic music.
